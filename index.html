Health Information & Guidelines

Healthy Diet Guidelines

  • Eat fresh, seasonal, and locally sourced foods whenever possible
  • Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables in your daily meals
  • Choose whole grains over processed foods
  • Limit sugar, salt, and processed food intake
  • Stay hydrated with adequate water intake (8-10 glasses daily)
  • Practice mindful eating and avoid overeating
  • Include healthy fats like nuts, seeds, and olive oil
  • Maintain regular meal timings

Healthy Lifestyle Recommendations

  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule (7-8 hours per night)
  • Exercise regularly for at least 30 minutes daily
  • Practice stress management techniques like meditation or yoga
  • Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption
  • Take regular breaks from screens and electronic devices
  • Spend time in nature and get adequate sunlight
  • Maintain social connections and relationships
  • Practice good hygiene and cleanliness

Astanga Wellness Practices

  • Start your day with gentle stretching or yoga poses
  • Practice deep breathing exercises (Pranayama) daily
  • Incorporate meditation into your routine
  • Follow the principles of Ahimsa (non-violence) in thought and action
  • Practice gratitude and positive thinking
  • Maintain balance in all aspects of life
  • Listen to your body's natural rhythms
  • Seek harmony between mind, body, and spirit

Disease Prevention Tips

  • Get regular health check-ups and screenings
  • Keep vaccinations up to date
  • Practice good hand hygiene
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Monitor blood pressure and cholesterol levels
  • Protect yourself from excessive sun exposure
  • Stay informed about seasonal health concerns
  • Seek early medical attention for persistent symptoms
