General Instructions — Diet & Lifestyle

Daily Routine (Dinacharya)

  • Wake up early (Brahmamuhurta) — gentle oiling and tongue scraping.
  • Light exercise or yoga, then warm water on empty stomach.
  • Regular meal times; prefer warm, freshly cooked food suitable to your constitution.
  • Avoid heavy, stale, and incompatible foods; reduce processed sugar and deep-fried items.

Lifestyle Tips

  • Sleep 6–8 hours; maintain a regular sleep schedule.
  • Manage stress with breathing techniques and meditation.
  • Follow doctor-prescribed herbal formulations and record any changes.
