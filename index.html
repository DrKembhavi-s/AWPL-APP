Daily Routine (Dinacharya)

  • Wake up before sunrise (5-6 AM)
  • Start day with warm water and meditation
  • Practice yoga or light exercise
  • Have regular meal times
  • Sleep by 10 PM for optimal rest

Dietary Guidelines

  • Eat fresh, seasonal, and local foods
  • Include all six tastes in meals
  • Avoid processed and frozen foods
  • Drink warm water throughout the day
  • Eat in a calm, peaceful environment

Mental Wellness

  • Practice daily meditation
  • Maintain positive relationships
  • Engage in meaningful activities
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Practice gratitude daily

Seasonal Care

  • Adjust diet according to seasons
  • Use appropriate oils for massage
  • Modify exercise intensity with weather
  • Follow seasonal detox practices
  • Dress according to climate

Ayurveda is the Ancient Indian Science of Life dating back to 5000 BC. It is a system which teaches harmonious living with nature through a balanced lifestyle of food and activities. It is a HOLISTIC science which addresses issues related to the BODY, MIND and SOUL. The main aim of Ayurveda is "TO MAINTAIN THE HEALTH OF A HEALTHY INDIVIDUAL AND TREAT THE DISORDERS OF AN UNHEALTHY INDIVIDUAL"

Ayurveda believes that everything in the universe is made up of FIVE ELEMENTS, called as the PANCHA MAHA BHUTAS. They are - Aakash, Vayu, Agni, Jala and Prithvi Mahabhuta. These are represented in the body as VATA, PITTA and KAPHA. Ayurveda sees the body as a micro representation of the Cosmos and therefore the factors that influence the environment have similar effects on the body.

Ayurveda is useful in a range of diseases - Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid Arthritis, Paralysis, Sciatica, Frozen Shoulder, Cervical and Lumbar Disc Problems, All Skin conditions especially Eczema & Psoriasis, Diabetes Mellitus, Hypertension, Obesity, High Cholesterol Levels, Thyroid Disorders, Menstrual Problems, Male and Female Infertility, Chronic Fatigue Syndrome, PCOD, Piles, Fistula-in-Ano, Fissure-in-Ano, Urinary Stones, Prostate Enlargement, Hair problems, and All Cosmetic problems.
